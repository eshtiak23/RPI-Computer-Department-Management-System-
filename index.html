About Department

Practical technical education for modern computing careers

The Department of Computer Science & Technology provides technical education focused on software, networking, hardware, programming, and hands-on learning. The department is designed to help students build practical skills that support higher study, industrial jobs, and modern career growth.

This homepage keeps everything simple and useful: official notices, basic department details, contact information, and easy access for students from both day and morning shift.
